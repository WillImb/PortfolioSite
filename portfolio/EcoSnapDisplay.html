EcoSnap

Overview

Explore a forest and capture pictures of animals in Ecosnap! Me and my teammate's submission for the 2025 Games For Change Student Challenge. Take photos of the local fauna and save them to your journal to learn more information about your find. This was a group project made in the spring of 2025.
Play Now On itch.io

Development

Brainstorming

We had about a month of time to work on our game and we decided to use Unity, because of our experience with it.
During the first half of the first week we brain stormed ideas.
The theme for the game had to be related to enviormental awarness.
We had ideas for a game where you play as a druid or as a bird.

Eventually we went with the idea of a photography exploring an animal filled wilderness.

My Work

  • Animal and world's 3D Models
  • Animal rigging, textures and animations
  • Animal movement and AI Logic
  • Player Movement
  • Taking and Saving photos
  • Level Creation

I coded everything in C# using Unity's framework and did all of the Animal models, textures and animations in Blender.
